The upcoming marriage of Grandula, High Elechon of the Belarans, and Sordani, Primaria of Tedorfell, will set aside centuries of strife to unite their kingdoms, establishing a new era of peace and allyship from Witchling Falls to the distant borders of the Cloudrift. In honor of this momentous occasion, the royal chronographer Sylphis Hedera has commissioned a new encyclopedia to record the joint history of their two kingdoms. They have assembled scholars and leximages from both kingdoms to put aside their differences and collaborate on a definitive historical account, starting from the mythical events of the Sundering Wars to the modern era.

The night Suta Aratelian was conceived his mother dreamed of a luminous bird with one head of a hawk and one head of an eagle. A clear sign that the fetal prince would one day become either a great king or a wise prophet. Determined to prevent the second scenario, the king ensured that young Aratelian grew up with no knowledge of human frailty or religious teachings.

When Aratelian learned the truth he fled his father's house and wandered the wilderness in search of wisdom. He learned to speak to the wind, the trees, and the Bhram itself. When he was satisfied he sought his home but found his father's kingdom had become a forgotten ruin.

And so it was that Aratelian crossed the Cloudrift to preach to the peasants of the Tedorfell frontier where he came to be known as Ratalan. Word quickly spread of his spiritual powers and perfect recall of the 374 Atakkra and the 7 secret Esakkra, despite lurid rumors spread by his enemies.

Ratalan first met the Primaria-Mother Jottess in 3656 when he presented her with an Icon of Harppe at the Valsitee. Ratalan shared with her eminence a vision of a jeweled serpent crushed under a red mule's heel. She was thus persuaded to spend the season in seclusion as she received personal spiritual instruction from Ratalan himself. No doubt, Jottess's remote location spared her during the Vermillion Days Attacks, and saved her eminence's life!

To this day Ratalan remains the Primeria-mother's closest, most trusted, and loyal advisor.
